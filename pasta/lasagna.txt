sauce
.5c chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
1 16oz can diced tomatos
1 8oz can tomato sauce
1 6oz can tomato paste
2ts dried basil
1 teaspoon salt

8oz lasagna noodles
1tbsp oil

2 eggs 
2.5c ricotta
.75c parmesan, grated
2tbsp dried parsley
1lb mozzarella

Cook onion and garlic. Stir in undrained tomatoes and other sauce ingredients. Cover, simmer 15m, stirring often.

Cook noodles until tender in boiling salted water with cooking oil added to water. Drain; rinse noodles.

Beat eggs; add ricotta, .5c of the parmesan, the parsley, 1tsp of salt, and .5tsp pepper. 

Layer half the noodles in a 13x9x2in baking dish; spread with half of hte ricotta filling. Add half of the mozzarella cheese and half of the sauce. Repeat layers. Sprinkle remaining parmesan on top.

Bake in a 375F oven for 30-35m or till heated through. Or assemble early and refrigerate; bake 45m or till hot. Let stand 10m. Makes 10 servings
