Minestrone Soup

Ingredients
1/2 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 medium onion, diced
6 cloves garlic, chopped
3 medium carrots, diced
2 large celery stalks, diced
2 medium yellow squash, diced
5 cups Vegetable Cooking Stock (could use more)
1/2 pound potatoes, peeled and diced (about 2-3)
1 bunch asparagus
1 pound spinach leaves cut into strips
1 cup orzo pasta
1 cup chunky chopped Italian plum tomatoes (about 2 plum tomatos)
2 bunches washed, julienned basil leaves
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons salt
Black pepper to taste
Fresh Parmesan for serving

Directions
Heat the olive oil in a heavy bottom soup pot.

Add in the onion and garlic and saute for 5 minutes or until the onion is soft.

Stir in celery, carrots, squash, asparagus and saute for 5 minutes more.

Add in the vegetable stock, potatoes, chard, spinach, pasta and tomatoes, and bring to a boil.

Lower heat and simmer for 10-12 minutes or until the potatoes are soft and the pasta has cooked.

Stir in the basil and Parmesan. Season to taste.

Divide between 8 warm soup bowls and grate some fresh Parmesan over each bowl.
