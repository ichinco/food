PASTINACA
1½ parts	Tequila Blanco
½ part		Del Maguey® Mezcal
1 part		St-Germain®
½ part		Freshly Squeezed Lime Juice
¾ part		Fresh Grapefruit Juice

First, one must think of oneself as an artisan. Then one must rim
rocks glass with chili salt (a blend of sea salt, chili powder and a
touch of cayenne pepper). Then fill said glass with ice. Finally,
shake all ingredients with ice and strain into glass. Bon! You are an
artisan.
