Ingredients

3 acorn squash
1 cup barleyqua
1/2 cup scallions
2 stalks of celery
2 tbsp pine nuts
2 tbsp fresh majoram
2 tsp olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp paprika

Cook the barley together with 3c of water.

Combine the barley, scallions, celery, pine nuts, majoram, oil, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl. 

Divde this mixture among the cavities of each squash. Sprinkle with paprika and bake 20-25m at 350d
or until squash is tender.
