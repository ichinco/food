1lb ground beef
onions
1 10.5oz can of cream of mushroom soup
salt and pepper to taste
1 16oz package of tater tots
2c shredded cheddar cheese

Preheat oven to 350F.

Brown ground beef in skillet until brown and crumbly 7-10m.

Stir in soup, season with salt and pepper.

Transfer beef mix to 9x13 baking dish. Layer tater tots evenly on top and sprinkle with cheddar cheese.

Bake in preheated oven until tater tots are golden brown and hot 30-45m.
