2-1/2c seasoned croutons (5oz pkg)
4eggs
2-1/4c milk
1 10-3/4oz can mushroom soup
1 10oz pkg frozen spinach, thawed, drained and squeezed
1 4oz can mushrooms, drained and chopped
1c shredded cheddar cheese
1c shredded monterey jack cheese
(or 1 pkg, 8oz, of combined shredded cheddar-jack
1/4tsp dry mustard

Spread croutons on bottom of greased 9x13 baking dish.

In lg bowl, whisk together eggs and milk until well blended. Stir in soup, spinach, mushrooms, cheeses,
and mustard. Pour egg mixture over croutons. Refrigerate overnite OR prepare 1 hour in advance.

Bake in preheated 325 oven 50-55 min or until set and lightly browned on top. May have to adjust for
size of party
