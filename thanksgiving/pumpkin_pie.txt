1 can (15oz) Libbey&#39;s 100% pure pumpkin
1 can (12oz) evaporated milk
3/4c granulated sugar
1/2tsp salt
1tsp ground cinnamon
1/2tsp ground ginger
1/4tsp ground cloves
2 lg eggs

1 unbaked 9-inch (4c volume) deep dish pie shell
Mix dry ingredients in small bowl. Beat eggs in large bowl. Stir in pumpkin and dry ingredient mixture.
Gradually stir in milk.
Pour into pie shell.
Bake in pre-heated 425 oven for 15 min. Reduce temp to350, bake for 30-40min or until knife inserted
in middle comes out clean. Cool on wire rack for 2 hours. Serve immediately or refrigerate.
Makes 10 servings. Add ice cream if desired.
