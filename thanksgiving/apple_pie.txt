2 crusts, see below
Filling
6c thinly sliced, peeled apples
3/4c sugar
2Tbsp flour
3/4tsp ground cinnamon
1/4tsp salt
1/8tsp ground nutmeg
1Tbsp lemon juice

Heat oven to 425. Place 1 crust in ungreased pie pan or dish. Press firmly against sides and bottom.

In large bowl, mix filling ingredients, spoon into crust. Top with second crust. Press edges together. Cut
slits to vent.

Bake 40-45min or until apples are tender and crust is golden brown. Cover edges after 15 minutes to
prevent burning. Cool on rack at least 2 hours before serving. 8 servings.

Add ice cream if desired.
