
Ingredients

1 (9 inch) pie shell
1 cup white sugar
1 cup light corn syrup
1/2 cup butter
4 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup bourbon
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon salt
6 ounces semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup chopped pecans


Directions

Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees F).

In a small saucepan combine sugar, corn syrup, and butter or
margarine. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until butter or
margarine melts and sugar dissolves. Cool slightly.

In a large bowl combine eggs, bourbon, vanilla, and salt. Mix
well. Slowly pour sugar mixture into egg mixture, whisking
constantly. Stir in chocolate chips and pecans. Pour mixture into pie
shell.

Bake in preheated oven for 50 to 55 minutes, or until set and
golden. May be served warm or chilled.
