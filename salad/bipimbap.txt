Spicy Red Pepper Paste Dressing

5 Tbsp Kochujang (Korean chili pepper paste)
1 Tbsp sugar
2 Tbsp honey
3 Tbsp rice wine vinegar
2 tsp minced garlic
1 tsp sesame oil

Mix all ingredients together until well-blended.
If sauce consistency is too thick, thin out with some warm water.
Use immediately or store in fridge.
