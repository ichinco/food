1 lb potatos
.5 lb green beans
.25 cup olive oil
2 tbsp white wine vinegar
1 tbsp dijon mustard
3/4 tsp worcestershire sauce
2 tbsp minced fresh dill or 2 teaspoons dried
.25 tsp salt
pinch of pepper

Cut the potatos into .5-in chunks. Steam the potatoes in a steamer for 10 minutes.

Meanwhile, cut the beans into 2-in lengths. 
Add the beans to the steamer, cook for 5-8 minutes longer.

Combine the oil, vinegar, mustard, worcestershire sauce, dill, salt, and pepper.

Toss the dressing with the potatoes and the green beans, serve the salad warm or
at room temperature.

Source: Easy Meals in Minutes.
