14oz diced tomatoes
5-6 garlic cloves
1tbsp minced ginger
1tsp turmeric
1tsp cayenne
1tsp paprika
2tsp garam masala
1tsp cumin
1tsp salt
4 oz butter
.5c cream or cocomilk
fresh cilantro

Add to instant pot: tomatoes, garlic, ginger, turmeric, cayenne, paprika, 1tsp garam masala,
cumin, salt, .25c water.

Cook on high pressure for 10m. Let the pressure release naturally.

Blend all ingredients using an immersion blender. 

Add butter cubes, cream, remaining 1tsp of garam masala, cilantro. Stir until incorporated.
Sauce should be thick enough to coat the back of a spoon.
