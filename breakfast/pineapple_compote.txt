Ingredients
1 pineapple
3/4c sugar or brown sugar
1.5c water

Cut pineapple into small cubes. 

Combine sugar and water in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil, ensure all sugar is dissolved.

Add pineapple to saucepan. Continue cooking until liquid is reduced, there's less liquid than pineapple (approx 10-20m).
