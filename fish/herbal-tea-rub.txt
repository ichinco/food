
citrus herbal tea rub


ingredients

1 cup green tea leaves
.5 cup dried peppermint, spearmint, or mint
1 cup lemongrass powder
2 tbsp sea salt
2 tbsp sugar
.25 cup ground ginger


directions

combine all the ingredients in a small bowl. use or store (for up to 3 weeks)

coat fish and saute.
